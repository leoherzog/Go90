The Go90 Scale of Doomed Streaming Services

wtf does this mean?

go90 was a failed streaming service by Verizon from 2015–2018. The Vergecast, the flagship podcast of The Verge, has a recurring joke based on this story. Each streaming service is given a rating between 0, meaning "no chance of going away any time soon", and 89, or "about to be shut down". Once a service is discontinued, it is said to have "gone 90".
